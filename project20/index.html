The first film, "Baahubali: The Beginning," introduces Shivudu, a young man with extraordinary strength who is raised by tribal villagers. He discovers his true identity as Mahendra Baahubali, the son of the late King Amarendra Baahubali. Through flashbacks, we learn that Amarendra was a beloved prince who was betrayed and killed by his cousin, Bhallaladeva, who became king. The second film, "Baahubali: The Conclusion," reveals Amarendra's backstory and the circumstances leading to his death. Amarendra marries Devasena, a fierce warrior princess, against Bhallaladeva's wishes. Bhallaladeva and his father conspire to imprison Devasena and kill Amarendra. Loyal subjects, including Kattappa, reluctantly carry out the assassination. Mahendra Baahubali returns to Mahishmati to avenge his father's death, free his mother, and reclaim the throne. He ultimately defeats Bhallaladeva in an epic battle, restoring peace and justice to the kingdom.
