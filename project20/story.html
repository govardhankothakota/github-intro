"Baahubali" is an epic tale set in the ancient kingdom of Mahishmati, focusing on the rivalry between brothers Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva for the throne. Amarendra, a beloved prince, is betrayed and killed by Bhallaladeva, who becomes king. Amarendra's son, Mahendra Baahubali, raised by villagers, learns of his heritage and returns to avenge his father's death. He frees his imprisoned mother, Devasena, and defeats Bhallaladeva in an epic battle. Mahendra reclaims the throne, restoring peace and justice to Mahishmati.
